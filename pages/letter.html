A Letter for Momo 💌

Words straight from my heart — just for you.

My dearest Momo,

I don't know if there are enough words in any language to fully describe what you mean to me — but I'm going to try, because you deserve every attempt.


From the moment you came into my life, something shifted. The world became a little brighter, a little warmer, and a whole lot more beautiful. You did that. You do that, every single day, just by being you.


I love the way you laugh — like it surprises even you sometimes. I love the way you care, deeply and quietly, without making a big deal of it. I love the way you make even the most ordinary moments feel like something worth remembering.


You've called me your "Parental Control" — and honestly, I wear that title with pride, because all it really means is that I care about you, I want to protect your heart, and I'll always be here keeping an eye out for you. Not to restrict you — but because watching over you and loving you is the greatest privilege I know.


I want you to know that on your hardest days, when you feel like you're not enough — you are. You are so much more than enough. You are the kind of person that people are lucky to know, and I am the luckiest of all.


Thank you for trusting me. Thank you for choosing me. Thank you for being my Momo. I promise to keep choosing you too — every single day, in every small and big way I can.


Forever yours,

Your Parental Control 💖
